Dictionary:

Dictionaries whether from general or specific purpose play the significant role in terms of informations about anything listed under the lexicon in the form of dictionary. Data base which is entered as a lexical entry in the dictionary involves various activities to represent exact scenario in terms of socio-cultural aspect of a language. Such informations are rich source of words and their synonyms, which are highly demanded for NLP tools and applications running on corpus. The content part of the dictionary may be presented in particular or general format and its format cannot specifically be bounded to a particular technology. Hence, two things are most remarkable; to represent particular subject matter there must be adequate dictionary format in the form of data model and another, it should be accessible to real users in such a way that they should be understandable, extendable, supportable and reusable with software tools. The present era is of language technology and Internet so it is imperative to develop Indian language technology which is accessible to masses through Internet. In order to make their dream true, many language experts and researchers are putting their best effort in academics and corporate sectors too. Presently, online dictionaries and machine translation systems are language orientated software for retrieval of lexical entries and translation output respectively by many users simultaneously. Presently, CSE, IIT (BHU) has developed bilingual and synset dictionaries for less resource languages like Magahi, Maithili and Bhojpuri.
